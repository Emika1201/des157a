INSTRUCTIONS

There are 2 players. If the turn lands on one of the players than they have to roll the dice Everytime each player rolls, the points are added together, unless either player's die has a "1". If one of the player's dice is a "1", it becomes the other player's turn. After each roll the player has a choice to "roll again" or "pass" if either player does not draw a "1". The first player that get 30 points or more is the winner.

If a player rolls two "1's"(snake eyes), then that player's points resets to zero and they have to start over.
